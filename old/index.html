Parallax Sections

ONE supports parallax background on every page section depending on cursor movement, and it also works on mobile devices.

Based on Twitter Bootstrap

We used sleek, intuitive and powerful font-end framework Twitter Bootstrap to create an excellent experience for users.

More than 400 font-awesome

ONE integrated with beautiful scalable vector icons from font awesome, you can easily include them in your posts.

600+ Google web fonts

Access the massive Google Fonts library and take control of your theme’s typography to create your unique style.

5 under construction options

E-commerce ready

Multiple pre-build pages

Responsive & retina display
